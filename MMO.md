# MMO

**Perliminary draft**

Working thought: Build may be a seed of a new category: a **possibly-first LLM-based MMO**.

MMO means **Massively Multiplayer Online game**, like *World of Warcraft*: many real people act together in a shared environment, while the game also contains non-player characters, systems, quests, tools, and progression.

In Build, the “world” is not fictional. The world is the real world. The players are the builders. The “NPCs” are AI agents. The quests are real projects. The progression is shipping real software, learning real tools, attracting real users, and creating real impact.

So maybe Build is not only a community or startup studio. It is a real-world, LLM-native multiplayer game where building is the gameplay.
