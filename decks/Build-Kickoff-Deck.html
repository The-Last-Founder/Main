Why we're here

Three goals

01

Learn by shipping

Use AI agents to build for startups — Cofounder.co, Claude, paperclip.ing and more. Hands on the keyboard, not slides.

02

Teach each other

Share what actually works — and what doesn't. The community's edge is its collective, real-world knowledge.

03

Launch a real MVP

Not a demo. A working open-source product with real users — built, deployed, and marketed together.

Our first project

The pilot: Johnny

What Johnny is

  • A WhatsApp-native agent that helps the Build community.
  • Our shared playground — everyone contributes to one real product.
  • Open to other ideas too: pilots get proposed and chosen together.

How we work

  • Start as a Cofounder.co project, with shared working principles.
  • Explore the agent tool stack as we go and write down what wins.
  • Pull requests welcome — that's how you join the build.

Revenue? Maybe. For now the goal is learning and real-world impact.
